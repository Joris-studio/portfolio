Nice to meet you. I'm Joris I'm a strategic product designer.


I've been designing intuitive interaction design for almost two decades. Loving B2B SaaS interfaces as those traditionally would be considered "complex" but wouldn't have to be. Where many would call those "dashboards" - I'd rather go for interaction than displaying. Is it not more useful to interact with data instead of just "displaying" it? Would action as such not bring the user further than just "being informed"?

I apply user-centric design to put an end to money- or ego driven discussions. Research will inform us. Opinions will inspire. The user decides. So working together with engineering and product, we're able to drive for the best of all worlds. Welcome to my site :)
